NEW STUDIES IDENTIFIED (7 Total)
================================

1. araujo-2019
   Title: Prevalence of Optimal Metabolic Health in American Adults
   Authors: Araújo, J., Cai, J., Stevens, J.
   Journal: Metabolic Syndrome and Related Disorders (2019)
   Sample: 16,914 US adults (NHANES 2009-2016)
   Key Finding: Only 12.2% of Americans metabolically healthy
   Quality Score: 11
   PMID: 30484738 | DOI: 10.1089/met.2018.0105
   → PRIMARY for q-means-framework

2. mottillo-2010
   Title: The Metabolic Syndrome and Cardiovascular Risk
   Authors: Mottillo, S., Filion, K.B., Genest, J., et al.
   Journal: Journal of the American College of Cardiology (2010)
   Sample: 951,083 (meta-analysis of 87 studies)
   Key Finding: MetS → 2× CVD risk, 1.5× mortality
   Quality Score: 12
   PMID: 20863953 | DOI: 10.1016/j.jacc.2010.05.034
   → PRIMARY for q-score-low

3. kramer-2013
   Title: Are Metabolically Healthy Overweight/Obesity Benign?
   Authors: Kramer, C.K., Zinman, B., Retnakaran, R.
   Journal: Annals of Internal Medicine (2013)
   Sample: 61,386 (meta-analysis of 8 studies, 10+ year follow-up)
   Key Finding: MHO → 1.24× CVD/mortality vs normal weight
   Quality Score: 11
   PMID: 24297192 | DOI: 10.7326/0003-4819-159-11-201312030-00008
   → SECONDARY for q-score-high, q-improve-metabolic

4. malmö-2003
   Title: Hyperinsulinemia as long-term predictor of death
   Authors: Ljungman, S., Wikstrand, J., Hartford, M., et al.
   Journal: Journal of Internal Medicine (2003)
   Sample: 6,074 Swedish men (19-year follow-up)
   Key Finding: Hyperinsulinemia → 1.58× mortality
   Quality Score: 9
   PMID: 12542553 | DOI: 10.1046/j.1365-2796.2003.01077.x
   → PRIMARY for q-insulin (second study)

5. karelis-2007
   Title: Relationship Between Inflammation & Insulin Resistance
   Authors: Karelis, A.D., St-Pierre, D.H., Conus, F., et al.
   Journal: Metabolism: Clinical and Experimental (2007)
   Sample: 38 obese women
   Key Finding: Establishes strict metabolic health criteria
   Quality Score: 8
   PMID: 17697862 | DOI: 10.1016/j.metabol.2007.03.011
   → SUPPORTING for q-trig-metabolic

6. wang-2023-metabolic
   Title: Plasma metabolites of healthy lifestyle & mortality
   Authors: Wang, Y., Sun, D., Vendemiale, G., et al.
   Journal: Medicine (2023)
   Sample: >100,000 (4 prospective cohorts, 20-28 year follow-up)
   Key Finding: Healthy metabolomic signature → 17% ↓ mortality, 25% ↑ longevity
   Quality Score: 12
   PMID: 36599867 | DOI: 10.1097/MD.0000000000032759
   → PRIMARY for q-score-high

7. reaven-1988
   Title: Banting Lecture 1988: Role of Insulin Resistance
   Authors: Reaven, G.M.
   Journal: Diabetes (1988)
   Sample: Conceptual/landmark paper
   Key Finding: Coined "Syndrome X"; insulin resistance central mechanism
   Quality Score: 13 (LANDMARK)
   PMID: 3056758 | DOI: 10.2337/diab.37.12.1595
   → PRIMARY for q-trig-metabolic, q-hdl-metabolic

8. glucose-diabetes-collaboration-2011
   Title: Glucose tolerance & cardiovascular mortality
   Authors: DECODE (Diabetes Epidemiology Collaborative Analysis)
   Journal: Diabetologia (2011)
   Sample: >1,000,000 (meta-analysis)
   Key Finding: Fasting glucose linear risk from normal range (no threshold)
   Quality Score: 11
   PMID: 21443997 | DOI: 10.1007/s00125-011-2144-2
   → PRIMARY for q-glucose
