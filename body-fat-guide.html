Women Significantly overweight

How to Measure Body Fat

DEXA Scan Most Accurate
Gold standard. Available at clinics and some gyms. ~$50-150 per scan.
Hydrostatic Weighing Very Accurate
Underwater weighing. Less common but highly reliable.
Calipers (Skinfold) Good
Manual measurement at multiple sites. Accuracy depends on technician skill.
Smart Scale (BIA) Moderate
Convenient for tracking trends. Can vary with hydration. Best used consistently.
Visual Estimation Rough Estimate
Compare to reference photos. Useful starting point, ±5% accuracy.
Tip: For the nutrition calculator, consistency matters more than precision. Pick one method and use it to track changes over time.
