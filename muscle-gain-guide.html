Understanding Anabolic Resistance

As we age, our muscles become less responsive to protein and training‚Äîa phenomenon called "anabolic resistance."

Age Anabolic Response Compensation Strategy
Under 40 Optimal response Standard protein (1.6-2.0 g/kg)
40-59 Mild-moderate resistance Higher protein per meal (25-30g+)
60+ Significant resistance Higher total protein (2.0-2.4 g/kg), 35-40g+ per meal
Good news: Anabolic resistance can be partially overcome with higher protein intake and consistent resistance training. You can still build muscle at any age‚Äîit just takes more effort and patience.
Research basis: These rates are derived from meta-analyses of resistance training studies. Individual results vary based on genetics, training history, and adherence to nutrition and training protocols.
